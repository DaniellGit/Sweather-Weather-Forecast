Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the Weather Forecast Web Application?
Simply visit our website and you'll land on the home page after you logged in, please make sure you already registered to use our features. From there, you can explore various features and input your location for personalized weather forecasts.
How can I get weather forecasts for my specific location?
On the homepage, you'll find a location search bar. Enter your city or region, and the application will provide you with accurate and localized weather predictions.
Is the service free to use?
Yes, Sweather Forecast Web Application is free to use. As long you registered, you are good to go.
What information does the application provide?
The Weather Forecast Web Application offers a range of information, including current temperature, rainfall forecasts, wind speed and direction, and air quality index.
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
